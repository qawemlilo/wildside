The Panda Campaign - A petition against the poaching of Africa's Rhino

Dear Sir or Madam

Please help stop the poaching of Africa's white and black rhino. We regard these creatures as one of our national treasures. Poaching for horn is having a devastating effect on our rhinos.

We feel much frustration and a great amount of helplessness with this current situation. We believe that if there was no demand for rhino horn in the Asian market, the poaching would end and our rhino could be saved. We ask you for your assistance as a person of influence.

Yours Sincerely

 

The campaign in Manderin
